The Nissan GT-R R35 is a high-performance sports car manufactured by Nissan,
first released in 2007 known for its cutting-edge technology, exceptional speed, and powerful engine,
earning it the nickname "Godzilla" in the automotive world.

About

  • It has a 3.8L Twin-Turbocharged V6 engine
  • It has a power output of 480hp
  • It does 0–60 mph (0–100 km/h): Approximately 2.9–3.5 seconds
